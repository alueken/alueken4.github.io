Project 1: Road Rush

In Road Rush, players control a car using arrow keys and avoid collisions with other cars. The game keeps the car within road boundaries, increases score over time, and uses randomly placed obstacles to simulate traffic.

Concepts Learned: for loops, object placement, randomization, key handling, and movement constraints.
